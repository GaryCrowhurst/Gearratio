Engine

The engine is the heart of your vehicle. It converts fuel into rotational energy measured in RPM (Revolutions Per Minute).

The engine's RPM is controlled by the accelerator pedal and regulated by the ECU (Engine Control Unit).

Most engines operate between 800 RPM (idle) and 6000-7000 RPM (redline).

Try adjusting the engine RPM slider to see how it affects the entire powertrain!
